Unit 1: Connect

Getting to know and establish the first connection with computer. Students learn to use computation as a benevolent medium for creative expression and interpersonal connection.

Keyword: conversation // interaction // expression

  • createCanvas
  • animation & interactivity
  • iteration

    • project 1: First Letter

    In this very first assignment, students will design an interactive visual card. The card is made with p5.js with at least one interactive and one animated components. The design is according to the content of the letter which students choose freely. Students are welcome to send it to the receiver using the link of their p5 editor. - Technical: - Shape, text, and other drawing functions. - Coordination in p5 sketch. - Flow control and animation. - How to share your p5 sketch. - Theme: - Meaningful interaction between visual and user: Back to the basis of mouse/keyboard and the screen. - Technology in a humane vision that connects people and serve as a vehicle for emotional and artistic expression.
